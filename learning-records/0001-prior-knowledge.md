Dominik has solid React/TypeScript experience but has not built a backend before. He has chosen his own tech stack through a grilling session (Hono, Drizzle, Neon, JWT) which means he has conceptual awareness of these tools but no hands-on experience yet.

**Implications:** Lessons should assume React/TypeScript is familiar ground and use it as an analogy anchor. Don't explain what a package.json is — but do explain what a Hono route is the first time it appears.
